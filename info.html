Things You Should Know

1. It's Not Just Hacking

Cybersecurity includes many domains like networking, risk analysis, compliance, security audits, cloud security, and more — not just hacking.

2. You Need Strong Basics

Before diving in, ensure you have a good understanding of:

  • Operating Systems (Linux/Windows)
  • Networking and Protocols (TCP/IP, HTTP, DNS)
  • Scripting (Python, Bash)

3. Soft Skills Matter

Communication, attention to detail, and continuous learning are essential to thrive in this field.

4. Certifications Can Help

Start with beginner-friendly certs like CompTIA Security+, CEH, or even Google’s Cybersecurity Certificate.
