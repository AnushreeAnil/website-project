🔐 Cybersecurity: Your Digital Shield
In today’s connected world, almost everything we do — from chatting with friends to shopping and banking — happens online. While the internet makes life easier, it also brings risks like hackers, viruses, and scams that can put your data and privacy in danger. That’s where cybersecurity comes in. It’s the protection we all need to stay safe in the digital world.

This website is designed for beginners who want to understand how to stay secure online. Here, you’ll explore:

  • What cybersecurity actually means
  • Why digital safety matters more than ever
  • Simple tips to protect yourself
  • The essential tools and techniques professionals use to keep systems secure

Whether you’re a student, a working professional, or just curious — this is your simple and smart starting point for learning the basics of cybersecurity, one click at a time. 🔐💡
